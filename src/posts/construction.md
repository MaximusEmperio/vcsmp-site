The Activity Team has decided to bring VCSMP back! Currently, the main contact is me (Emperio). I should be done configuring everything this week :D

As usual, VCSMP will be an unmodded server ("vanilla", if you will). There are server-side plugins, but these are largely unintrusive and don't fundamentally change the game. We are introducing light elements of _Towny_, however, mostly for how it lets players organise into towns/nations and handles claims/plots for everyone.
